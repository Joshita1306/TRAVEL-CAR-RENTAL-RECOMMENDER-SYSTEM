✈️ Travel Tips & Advice

Packing Essentials

Always pack medications in your carry-on, bring versatile clothing, and don't forget universal adapters for electronics.

Money Saving Tips

Travel during shoulder seasons (just before/after peak) for lower prices and fewer crowds while still enjoying good weather.

Local Experiences

Try authentic local food markets instead of tourist restaurants for better prices and more genuine cultural experiences.

Safety Advice

Photograph your passport and important documents, and email copies to yourself as backup in case of loss or theft.

Family Travel

Pack a small first-aid kit and favorite snacks. Schedule downtime between activities when traveling with children.
